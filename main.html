Weighted grade calculation

The weighted grade formula is calculated by multiplying each grade (g) by its corresponding
weight (w), and then dividing the sum of these products by the total weight:

Weighted grade = (w1× g1 + w2× g2 + w3× g3+...) / (w1 + w2 + w3 +...)
E.g 25% weight with a grade of 90% for assignments
50% weight with a grade of 74% for midterms
25% weight with a grade of 88% for quizzes
Weighted grade = (25×90 + 50×74 + 25×88) / (25+50+25) = 81.50%

Final exam grade calculation

The final exam grade needed to achieve a certain grade is calculated by taking the target grade (t),
subtracting the product of (1 - the weight of the final exam (w)) times the current grade (g),
and then dividing by the weight of the final exam:

(All percentages are divided by 100)

Final exam grade = ((t - (1 - w) * g) / w) × 100
E.g Your current grade is 70%
You want a grade of 80%
The final exam is 50% of the final grade
Final exam grade needed to achieve goal = ((0.8 - (1 - 0.5) × 0.7) / 0.5) × 100 = 90%
